About CRS Automotive

Based in Jamaica, New York, CRS Automotive Parts, Inc. has built a reputation as a trusted manufacturer and distributor of OE quality automotive parts for the trade.

We specialize in the drivetrain components that keep vehicles moving — hub bearing assemblies, prop shaft assemblies, and CV axles. Every part we supply is held to original equipment manufacturer specification, so your customers get the fit, feel and longevity they expect the first time.

Whether you're a repair shop, wholesale distributor or fleet operator, CRS offers competitive pricing, reliable stock and the technical expertise to match the right part to the right application.
