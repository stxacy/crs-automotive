About CRS Automotive

Manufacturer and distributor of OE quality automotive parts, trusted by repair shops and distributors across the region.

Who We Are

CRS Automotive Parts, Inc. is a manufacturer and distributor of OE quality automotive parts based in Jamaica, New York.

We built our reputation by focusing on what we do best — the drivetrain and bearing components that form the backbone of every vehicle. Hub bearing assemblies, prop shaft assemblies, and CV axles are our core specialisms, complemented by a full range of sensors and ignition components.

Every part we supply is manufactured to original equipment specification. That means correct fitment, correct materials, and the performance your customers expect — first time, every time.

We work with repair shops, wholesale distributors and fleet operators across the New York area and beyond. Our team has the technical knowledge to match the right part to the right application, and the stock to back it up.
